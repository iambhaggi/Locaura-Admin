1. Rider Registers with phone number and name
2. Document Pan , license, vehicle details, Rider photo, Bank details.
3. Admin verifies the documents and approves the rider.
4. Rider can login and set his status to online and available.
5. When a order is generated, it is assigned to the nearest available rider.
6. Rider can accept or reject the order.
7. If accepted, rider can navigate to the retailer location and pick up the order.
8. Once the order is picked up, the rider can navigate to the customer location and deliver the order.
